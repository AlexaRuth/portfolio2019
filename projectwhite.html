PROBLEM STATEMENT

There are many sites that have been established several several years ago. As time has passed the disipline of web design has changed with new standards and new ways of approaching web design. However many sites in the past remain untouched and worse yet are not able to be updated easily.

SOLUTION

For White Birch resorts the solution was to modernize the site a little bit and bring some much needed white space into thier look and feel. With this new layout the hope is that the resort would look more premium, information will become easier to find and people will have a clearer understanding of who White Birch is.
