Individuality . Sustainability . Quality

We are a textile dealer with office and finishing production located in the Rhine-Main area(close to Frenkfurt Airport). Providing a wide range of products, we stand for qualitive, sustainable embrodiery and print finishings in single piece or bulk productions, for private or corporate clients.

Allround service

We are happy to assist you in every matter - starting with the creation of your logo or the textile selection up until your final purchase order is completed to your needs and likes. Feel free to contact us voia phone, email or directly at our location in Russelsheim.

Quality

Due to extensive experience we can offer qualitative perfection in each of our products - none leave the house without a thorough quality control which can also be witnessed along the whole line of production.

Social responsibility

Our embroideries are made to 100% in Germany. When slecting materials a fair and clean production are key in the decision making process. Equally, when choosing out buisness partners, it is imperative that thier values and actions suit Wailua's point of responsibility.

Flexibility

We meet challeneges with enthusiasim and creativity. We love to improve ourselves and will try to find for every clients' wish and individual solution.

Textile Finishing

Since embroidery – the most sophisticated textile finishing - is our area of expertise we can guarantee longevity, wash resistance and durability of all our embroidered products. We also offer screen printing and other top-quality textile finishings.
