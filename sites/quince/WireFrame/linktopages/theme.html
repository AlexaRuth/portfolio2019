Quince Themes

Traditional

Usually this type of party is thrown at your parents or close relatives place. The style of the party is like the name traditional with traditional foods and decor. Decor is usually home-made and help prepared by many family members. Many prefer this type of setting because its intimate and gets the whole family involved in this special day.

alt="placeholder">

Black and White

A classic theme used as a style of sophistication. Many variations exist for example red and gold or navy and silver. Unlike a traditional style this one is one you expect everyone to dress accordingly.

alt="placeholder">

Favorite Color

When will you ever get to have a party of your favorite color?! Right! giving your childhood clichés for a run why not celebrate with keeping the scheme simple with your favorite color. This is one of the most popular themes blending childhood to adulthood seamlessly.

alt="placeholder">

Theme

Under the Sea, Arabian Nights, Winter wonderland, Candyland You Name it. As of recently and with the rise of Pinterest themed Quinceañera's are on the rise. These parties are super fun to plan however can be really time consuming. If time is on your side definitely think about one of these fun filled events.
